Popularity history is calculated using the percentage of matches each hero was picked or banned (depending on the tab) in every 200 professional matches with a Liquipedia tier of 1 - 3. For example, if the "Picks and First Phase Bans" plot for Abaddon is at 0.156 on January 19th, 2016, it means that Abaddon was either picked or banned in the first phase in 15.6% of the 200 tier 1 - 3 matches leading up to January 19th.
